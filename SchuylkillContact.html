FAQ

Q: What is your organization?

A: We are a volunteer run non-profit organization that is concern with with the enivorment and how it affects our river?

Q: How do I get involved with Save the Schuylkill River?

A: Visit our Event page to sign up. You can also follow us through our social media sites. If you cann't come to of our events you can donate to our cause.

Q: What donatations do you accept?

A: First all you can donate your time and effort! That is a big help in cleaning up the Schuylkill River. You can also donate money or any equipment that is needed.

Q: What is your ultimate goal?

A: We want a clean Schuykill River that will be enjoyed through the centuries. We want make it substainable for the future.

Q: What sort of events do you hold?

A: We have a weekly river cleaning every Saturday afternoon. We also have a larger events with informational session and fundraising event at local festivials. We also participate in the Dragon Boat Race Team. You have a wide range of events to get involve.

Q: How do my company/group get involve with your events?

A: You can email us at groupssavetheschuylkill@gmail.com for groups that are larger that twenty. We will help you find the best way to help Save the Schuykill River.
