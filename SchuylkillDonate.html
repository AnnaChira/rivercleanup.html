Donate your time to Schuykill River

If you like to get involve with our weekly cleanup of the Schuylkill River. You can visit our events page to sign up.

Donate much need equipment

We accept much needed epuipment to help with cleaning. We accept: trash bags, work gloves, shovels and old clothes.

Donate Money for #Save the Schuylkill River!

If you can't come to one of our events. You can donate to our fund the Schuykill River Fund. We have many donation levels.

When you donate to the #Save the Schuylkill River! You will be part of the club that helps further our mission. We have many donation levels that indicate what level.

  • Up to $50: Love Park
  • Up to $100: Ben Franklin Parkway
  • Up to $500: The Art Museum
  • Up to $1000: The Schuykill River Trail
  • Up to $5,000: Fairmont Park

If your company or group like to help sponsor to #Save the Schuylkill River.

Please email us at savetheriver@gmail.com. We have donation levels that indicate levels. We have levels of sponsorship with Save the Schuylkill River.

  • Up to $5,000: Wissahickon Creek
  • Up to $10,000: Delaware River
  • Up to $20,000: Schuykill River

About Donations

All of your donations to Save the Schuylkill River is tax deductiable.

For corporate sponsorship is part of the larger Save the Schuylkill River.

Any other questions that you have about your donations you can email us at savetheriver@gmail.com and we will respond in a timely manner.
